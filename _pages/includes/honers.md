# Open science, software and service
- *2020.12* [ByteDance Scholars Program](https://ur.bytedance.com/scholarship) (10 students in China each year)
- [HydroModPy](https://github.com/HydroModPy/HydroModPy/tree/dev-refact) is an open-source Python toolbox for catchment-scale shallow groundwater modelling, developed for research, reproducible workflows and teaching.
- The Planetary Evolution Model hydrology component extends my modelling work to early Mars, where I develop high-resolution simulations of lakes, overflow pathways and water redistribution.
- I contribute to collaborative research groups including the Mars Through Time ERC team, the HydroModPy development team, RIVAGES Normands 2100 and earlier activities linked to Future Earth.
- My service activities include peer review for *Hydrogeology Journal* and *Geoscientific Model Development*.
- I regularly organise or animate collective work around research software, including HydroModPy development meetings, refactoring workshops and conference-related activities within Mars Through Time.
