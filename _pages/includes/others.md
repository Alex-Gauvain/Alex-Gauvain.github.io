# Teaching, supervision and background

## Teaching and supervision

- GIS practical sessions in the Master's programme in Water Sciences at the University of Rennes, with a strong emphasis on hydrological-risk mapping and spatial analysis.
- Field supervision for Master students in Normandy and Brittany, linking measurements, site interpretation and hydrogeological reasoning.
- Informal HydroModPy training for interns, PhD students and postdoctoral researchers since 2022, focused on reproducible modelling workflows.
- Supervision of research internships on early Mars hydrology, reconstructed palaeotopography and web tools for hydrogeological modelling.

## Education and experience

- Postdoctoral researcher at the Laboratoire de Meteorologie Dynamique, CNRS / Sorbonne Universite, since 2022.
- Research engineer in hydrogeological modelling at Geosophy in 2022.
- Research engineer at Geosciences Rennes from 2017 to 2019, with work on coastal groundwater hazards and the RIVAGES Normands 2100 programme.
- PhD in Earth and Environmental Sciences from Universite de Rennes 1, completed in 2022.
- MSc in Earth and Environmental Sciences (Hydro3) from Universite de Rennes 1, completed in 2016.
